## Project Overview

For this case study, I designed a UX strategy for a startup developing a new mobile app.
The goal of this application is to help individuals with chronic health conditions manage their medications and doctor appointments.
The app allows users to set reminders, track their medication usage, and communicate with healthcare professionals.
